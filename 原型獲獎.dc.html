原型獲獎

獲獎是對方法的肯定

每一項獎項的背後，都是對設計方法與態度的回應。從構築的實驗精神到細節的精準掌握，我們相信「獲獎」並非終點，而是對思考深度與執行力的再確認。

Recognition as a Validation of Method

Every award reflects a response to our design method and attitude. From structural experimentation to precise detailing, recognition is not an endpoint but an affirmation of thought and execution.

以作品對話世界

原型結構的作品多次榮獲國內外獎項，包括 ADA 新銳建築獎、金點設計獎 與 iF 設計獎。這些肯定代表建築不僅是形式的創新，更是理念與社會價值的連結。每一件作品皆以清晰的結構語言與人文關懷，持續在世界舞台上發聲。

Dialogue Through Work

Prototype Structure has received multiple honors, including the ADA Emerging Architect Award, the Golden Pin Design Award, and the iF Design Award. These achievements show that architecture is not only about form but also about connecting ideas with cultural and social values. Each project communicates through structural clarity and human sensitivity, engaging in a dialogue with the world.

從榮譽回到原點

對我們而言，榮譽的意義在於回望過程。每一次被看見，都是一次重新出發——讓團隊持續檢視建築的初衷，回到材料、結構與時間的對話，從實踐中再發現創作的起點。

Returning to the Origin

For us, honor means reflection. Each recognition is a moment to look back, refine our process, and rediscover the origins of creation. Through every project, we return to the dialogue between material, structure, and time—continuing to seek meaning beyond the award itself.
